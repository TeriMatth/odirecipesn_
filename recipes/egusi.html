EGUSI SOUP

DESCRIPTION

Egusi Soup is an outstandingly delicious, quick, and easy soup with ground egusi seeds, similar to pumpkin seeds. This meaty, nutty, spicy soup is protein-packed and full of tasty greens.

INGREDIENTS

    Egusi seed Beef Smoke fish Beef tripple
  • Crayfish
  • Spinach
  • Peper
  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Tomatoes

Steps for preparing Egusi soup

  1. Boil the meat seasoned with salt, garlic, onions untill meat is tender.
  2. Saute onions, meat and crayfish over a medium heat.
  3. Add tomatoes and stir.
  4. Add the grounded egusi and steam for about ten minutes.
  5. Add spinach ana allow to steam for five minutes
  6. Serve hot
