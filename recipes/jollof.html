DESCRIPTION

JOLLOF RICE: What is it? Rice made with tomatoes, onions, scotch bonnets, and chili peppers. There are endless variations on this recipe, but those five ingredients are usual constants.

No list of Nigerian foods can be complete without a mention of Jollof rice. It's a party dish brought out at celebrations, a signature regional dish, and the recipe with which many West African communities — and countries — like to stir up friendly rivalries. If I had to pick just one favorite from this entire list, this one would probably be it

Ingredients for making Jollof rice

  • 4 cups of rice
  • 1kg Chicken/beef
  • 1000ml of Ground Fresh tomatoes.
  • 50ml Ground Fresh peppers.
  • 1 Teaspoon Curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon Thyme
  • 1 teaspoon Nutmeg powder
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 cup of sliced onions
  • 300ml vegetable
  • salt to taste.
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 fingers of ginger
